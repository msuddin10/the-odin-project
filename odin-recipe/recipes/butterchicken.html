Step by step guide

  1. Mix chicken, lemon juice, garlic, garam masala, and salt. Marinate 30 mins (or overnight in fridge).
  2. Sauté onions in oil over medium until golden (about 20 mins). Add garlic, cook 1 min.
  3. Stir in garam masala, paprika, cinnamon, and salt. Cook 1 min. Add tomatoes, cook 2 mins.
  4. put the fried onion on top of the cooked chicken
  5. Add cream. Blend until smooth. Simmer gently.
  6. Put the boiled rice on top
  7. put Safron
  8. Stir in marinated chicken. Cook covered 12 mins, until done.
  9. cook for a little more
  10. tir in butter. Adjust salt. Garnish with cilantro if you want. Serve with warm, fluffy garlic naan, cheese naan or rice and enjoy!
